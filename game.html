Inifinity Life: Gaming Websites(Responsive)

Project description

UX Case Study

Duration: 14th May - 18th May

Product Overview

Inifinity Life is a dynamic gaming website for gamers and industry enthusiasts. Targeting gamers and aspiring developers, Inifinity Life provides curated content, engaging features, and a vibrant community.


Problem

Hard for gamers to stay updated on games, industry news, product launches, and career opportunities.


Goal

Inifinity Life aims to be the premier hub for gamers, integrating news, updates, reviews, and career resources.

Roles and Responsibilities

My Role

UI/UX Designer: Responsible for the overall design and user experience for Inifinity Life

Responsibility

My responsibility involves conducting user research, designing wireframes, developing the app's features, testing and iterating, and implementing the final design and functionality.

User research: Summary

My user research for "Inifinity life" involved a mix of qualitative and quantitative methods to understand our target audience's needs and preferences. Initially, I made assumptions about gamers' interests and online behaviors, but my research revealed a stronger emphasis on community engagement, user-generated content, and career development resources than we had anticipated. These insights helped us tailor the platform to better serve our users' needs and expectations.

Usability Study

Usability Study:Parameters

  • Navigation Efficiency:Assessing how easily users can find and access various sections of the website.
  • Content Accessibility: Evaluating how effectively users can consume and understand the provided content.
  • Engagement and Interaction: Measuring how users interact with community features and participate in discussions.

Usability Study:Findings

  • Improved Navigation: Users found the main navigation intuitive, but suggested clearer categorization and labeling of sections for quicker access.
  • Content Readability: While users appreciated the quality of the content, they recommended enhancing readability by using larger fonts and more visual aids.
  • Enhanced Community Features: Participants enjoyed the community interaction but desired more engaging features like live Q&A sessions and better forum organization to foster deeper connections.

Accessibility

Scalable Text and Fonts

  • I implemented adjustable text sizes and clear, legible fonts to accommodate users with varying levels of visual acuity.

    • Subtitles and Captions

  • All video content includes subtitles and captions to assist users with hearing impairments in understanding the audio content.

    • Consistent Layout and Navigation

  • The design maintains a consistent and intuitive layout, aiding users with cognitive disabilities in navigating and using the platform without confusion.
